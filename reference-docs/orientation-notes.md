The MSME Business Needs track (Problem Statement 3) is designed to address the large, unserved and underserved segment of the MSME sector, which is described as the backbone of the Indian economy 

Here is the end-to-end breakdown of the challenge:

The Objective: The goal is to co-create a solution using AI/ML tools to identify the specific business requirements of MSME customers to provide them with tailored business loan offerings (11:05-11:18).
Data Sources: Participants should utilize data that is readily available or accessible without initial borrower consent, including:
GST details for business activity insights (10:49-10:54).
Account Aggregator (AA) framework to consolidate liability account details from other banking institutions (10:56-11:02).
The Process: By consolidating these diverse data points using AI/ML, the solution should build a comprehensive profile to identify business needs that the bank can meet through credit facilities (11:02-11:17).
Expected Outcome: A digital product that effectively maps customer needs to specific loan products, enabling the bank to penetrate a segment that has historically been difficult to reach (11:18-11:38).
